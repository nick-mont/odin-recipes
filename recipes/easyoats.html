Easy Overnight Oats

This recipe will teach you how to make a basic overnight oats recipe for beginners.

You'll also find some tips for meal prepping them in batches,

and some topping inspiration with four popular overnight oatmeal flavors.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Almond Milk
  • Chia Seeds
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Protein Powder
  • Sweetener (optional):
    • Sugar
    • Honey
    • Maple Syrup
    • Stevia
  • Toppings (optional)
    • Nuts/Nut Butters
    • Fresh Fruit
    • Dried Fruit
    • Seeds and Spices

Steps

  1. Combine 3/4 cup of old fashioned oats and 2tbsps of chia seeds in a bowl, mason jar, or some glass cup.
  2. Add a scoop of protein powder
  3. Add 1/4tsp or 1/2tsp of vanilla extract
  4. Add 3/4 cup of almond milk and mix the overnight oats until there is no clumps
  5. Add optional sweetener
  6. Next, seal or cover the lid, and let chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight
  7. After the wait, add in whichever toppings you plan on using and enjoy!
